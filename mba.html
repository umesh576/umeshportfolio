Eligibility for admission in MBA Program

Students seeking admission to MBA Program must have completed Bachelor level in any discipline having minimum 45% or 2.00 CGPA from the university recognized by Purbanchal University, Nepal. Admission Intake: Fall session (July / August) 30 seats and Spring Session (January/February) 30 seats.
