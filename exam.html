Message From Head of Exam Section

Er. Pushkar Sharma

The Examination Section at HWIC plays a crucial role in ensuring the smooth conduct of examinations, from planning and scheduling to result processing. Our team is dedicated to upholding the highest standards in assessment and evaluation, adhering to the guidelines and regulations set forth by the college and relevant educational authorities. Our commitment to providing a conducive and transparent examination environment is paramount in fostering a culture of learning and growth.

We understand that examinations can be stressful time for students, and it is our aim to create an atmosphere that minimizes anxiety and encourages optimal performance. To achieve this, we strive to maintain a secure and confidential examination process, treating each student's academic journey with the utmost respect and confidentiality.

As a student-centric institution, we continuously work to improve our examination systems, incorporating feedback and best practices to enhance the overall assessment experience. We believe in fairness and impartiality, ensuring that every student's hard work is duly recognized and rewarded.
